We Love Great Food.

And it just so happens to be vegan! What started as a simple idea, to show people how delicious vegan food can truly be, has since turned into a global platform celebrating plant-based cooking and everything the vegan lifestyle has to offer.

Whether or not you are vegan, we hope you’ll find plenty of inspiration and new ideas here. Best of Vegan could not exist without its amazing readers, featured bloggers, chefs, restaurants, cookbook authors, editors, and contributors.
